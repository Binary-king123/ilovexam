About SAT 2027

The SAT is an internationally recognized standardized test for college admissions, widely accepted by US and international universities. SAT 2027 is fully digital and tests Reading, Writing, and Math.

SAT 2027 Exam Pattern

  • Mode of Exam: Digital
  • Sections: Reading & Writing, Math
  • Total Duration: 2 Hours 14 Minutes
  • Score Range: 400�1600
  • No negative marking for wrong answers

Preparation Tips

  • Strengthen reading comprehension and grammar for R&W section.
  • Master algebra, data analysis, and problem solving for Math.
