About CUET 2027

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) is conducted for admission to various UG and PG programs in central universities and other participating institutions in India.

CUET 2027 Exam Pattern

  • Mode of Exam: Online (Computer Based)
  • Sections: Language, Domain Subjects, General Test
  • Total Questions: Varies by subject selection
  • Marking Scheme: +5 for correct, -1 for wrong
  • Exam Duration: 2-3 Hours (based on chosen subjects)

Preparation Tips

  • Focus on your domain subjects and language skills.
  • Practice general reasoning and current affairs for the General Test.
  • Attempt topic-wise mock tests for better accuracy.
