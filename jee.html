About JEE 2027

The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) is the gateway for admission to top engineering institutes like IITs, NITs, and IIITs. JEE 2027 is conducted in two phases: JEE Main and JEE Advanced.

JEE 2027 Exam Pattern

  • Mode of Exam: Online (Computer Based)
  • Total Questions: 90 (75 to be attempted in JEE Main)
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Marking Scheme: +4 for correct, -1 for wrong
  • Exam Duration: 3 Hours

Preparation Tips

  • Practice topic-wise mock tests and previous year papers.
