About NEET 2027

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is a medical entrance exam for admission into MBBS, BDS, and AYUSH courses across India. NEET 2027 is conducted by NTA (National Testing Agency).

NEET 2027 Exam Pattern

  • Mode of Exam: Offline (Pen & Paper)
  • Total Questions: 200 (180 to be attempted)
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Marking Scheme: +4 for correct, -1 for wrong
  • Exam Duration: 3 Hours 20 Minutes

Preparation Tips

To score well in NEET 2027:

  • Focus on NCERT books for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • Practice mock tests and previous year question papers.
  • Manage time effectively and revise regularly.
